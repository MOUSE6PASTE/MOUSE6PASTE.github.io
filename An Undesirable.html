An Undesirable

An Undesirable is a deduction game about playing as a fascist investigator

You can play on itch.io

I did all the writing/programming and my girlfriend made all of the art

Made in two days for Lame Jam 62 with a theme of Censorship. You use documents provided to find out who in a given image needs to get censored and why

I really like that style of game, and I've been fiending to make one, so I got some of that out here but I still want to do more...

It's supposed to be a play on that poem about people who live in a fascist government getting screwed over for not standing up sooner
